# Data Storytelling Skill

Transform raw data into compelling narratives that drive decisions and inspire action.

## Core Frameworks

### 1. Story Structure
- **Setup**: Context and baseline metrics.
- **Conflict**: The current problem or hidden opportunity identified in the data.
- **Resolution**: Insights, recommendations, and clear next steps.

### 2. The Narrative Arc
1. **Hook**: Grab attention with a surprising insight (e.g., "We are losing $2M to churn").
2. **Context**: Establish the baseline (industry standards vs our current performance).
3. **Climax**: The key data insight that explains the current situation.
4. **Action**: Clear, prioritized recommendations with expected ROI.

## Ecommerce Use Cases

- **Quarterly Business Reviews (QBR)**: Transform GMV/ROI charts into growth stories.
- **Campaign Retrospects**: Show "what worked" and "what failed" with clear causal links.
- **Investment Pitches**: Use "Progressive Reveal" to demonstrate business flywheel potential.
- **Emergency Root Cause Reports**: Explain why metrics (like return rate) shifted and how to fix it.

## Best Practices

- **Start with the "So What"**: Lead with the single most important insight.
- **Rule of Three**: Keep it to three main points or comparisons for maximum impact.
- **Annotate Charts**: Don't just show a line; point to where the strategy changed.
- **Call to Action**: Every data story must end with a specific decision or next step.
